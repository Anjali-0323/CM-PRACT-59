### 🍲 Simple Recipe Text (Tea Recipe)

**Tea Recipe**

Ingredients:

* 1 cup water
* 1 cup milk
* 1 teaspoon tea powder
* 1 teaspoon sugar

Steps:

1. Boil water in a pan.
2. Add tea powder and let it boil.
3. Add milk and sugar.
4. Boil for 2–3 minutes.
5. Strain and serve hot.
